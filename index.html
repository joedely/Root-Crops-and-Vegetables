About Root Crops

Root crops grow underground and are rich in carbohydrates, fiber, and important vitamins. They are staple foods in many countries.

About Green Vegetables

Green leafy and pod vegetables are excellent sources of vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants. They help boost the immune system and keep the body healthy.

Why Vegetables Matter

Both root crops and green vegetables are essential for a balanced diet. Farmers grow them to support nutrition, food security, and healthy living.
